Fried Rice

Description

Like most Asian countries, Japan has its own version of fried rice. It’s called Yakimeshi (焼きめし), and it literally means pan-fried rice.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • ham
  • eggs
  • salt
  • black peeper
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Use your hands to crumble the rice.
  2. Heat a pan and then add the oil, swirling to coat the pan.
  3. Add the egg.
  4. Add the ham, and toss to distribute evenly.
  5. Drizzle the soy sauce.
  6. To serve the Yakimeshi, you can press the rice into a bowl
