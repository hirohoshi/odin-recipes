Apple Cinnamon Zucchini Muffins

Description

A moist, dense and delicious snack. Super easy! I had apples and zucchini that were just begging to be turned into something wonderful!

Ingredients

  • cup oil
  • eggs
  • flour
  • apple
  • soda
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Spray 24 muffin cups with cooking spray
  3. Whisk sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth
  4. Stir flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and baking powder into sugar mixture just until batter is combined.
  5. Fold zucchini and apple into batter and spoon into the prepared muffin cups.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until until muffins spring back after pressing the top, about 25 minutes.
